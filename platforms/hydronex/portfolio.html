03Governance

Structure.

Obsidian as Principal.

Obsidian Capital Partners holds principal economic and governance positions in Hydronex via the single-asset fund vehicle established Q3 2024. The relationship is structural, not transactional.

OCP appointees serve on the Hydronex board alongside operating leadership. Decision rights, reserved matters, and information rights are defined in the operating agreement.

Operating leadership.

Day-to-day operations are directed by Hydronex's CEO and operating team. The HydroCore technology arm operates under separate technical leadership with full alignment to the Hydronex platform.

Material decisions follow the gated framework defined at the OCP master level: every escalation is structured, every commitment is documented, every milestone is owned.
