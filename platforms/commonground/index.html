Telemedicine · Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Commonground.
Care, made continuous.

"The shortest distance between patient and practitioner is the one we are building."

Commonground builds telemedicine infrastructure for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia under Vision 2030 frameworks. We connect patients to clinicians across geography, language, and continuity of care — built for clinical gravity, regional regulatory depth, and the long arc of population health.

01Market Context

Vision 2030, made clinical.

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is undertaking one of the most ambitious healthcare modernization programs in the world. Commonground is built specifically for the structural opportunity this creates — not adapted from a Western telehealth model.

01

Population health at scale

Saudi Arabia's healthcare modernization spans urban centers, secondary cities, and remote regions. Telemedicine is not a feature — it is infrastructure, and the demand is structural.

02

Regulatory framework, in motion

NHC and SDAIA frameworks define the regulatory environment for digital health. Commonground is built to operate within them — not around them. Compliance is a feature.

03

Care continuity, across distance

A patient seen once should not be a patient seen once. Commonground architecture treats every encounter as part of a longitudinal record — across providers, geographies, and languages.

02Platform

Built for clinical gravity.

Commonground is not a video-visit product. It is the infrastructure beneath continuous care — patient identity, clinical record, encounter routing, prescription continuity, and outcomes measurement, designed as one system.

The brand stands deliberately apart from the Western "consumer telehealth" archetype. Patient outcomes are the measure. Engagement metrics are a means, never an end.

Bilingual by design — Arabic and English as primary languages, with the brand book's mandate that Arabic typography be treated as a primary typeface, not a translation afterthought. IBM Plex Arabic anchors the regional design system.

Every KSA-facing material reviewed by regional legal before publication. Every clinical claim grounded in NHC framework. This is the discipline the audience expects, and the standard we hold ourselves to.
